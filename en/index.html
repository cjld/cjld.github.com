Dun Liang（梁盾）

NVIDIA · Senior Engineer, AI DevTech

Dun Liang, from Changsha, Hunan, was admitted to Tsinghua University's Department of Computer Science through the Informatics Olympiad. He won an ACM-ICPC regional gold medal and championships in ISC/SC international student supercomputing competitions. During his Ph.D. under Academician Shi-Min Hu, he led the development of the Jittor deep learning framework (launched in 2018), the first framework supporting dynamic compilation, with related projects exceeding 10,000 stars on GitHub. After graduation, he founded and served as CEO of Beijing Fitten Technology, driving multiple AI Infra and AI Coding products to production. He is currently a Senior Engineer in NVIDIA DevTech, focusing on LLM training performance optimization and Megatron Core development.

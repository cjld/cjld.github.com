jekyll如果出现错误Liquid Exception: Invalid Byte Sequence in GBK in ***，需要改以下两处：

#C:\Ruby200-x64\lib\ruby\gems\2.0.0\gems\jekyll-1.2.1\lib\jekyll\converter.rb
self.content = File.read(File.join(base, name), :encoding => "utf-8")
#C:\Ruby200-x64\lib\ruby\gems\2.0.0\gems\jekyll-1.2.1\lib\jekyll\tags\include.rb
source = File.read(File.join(includes_dir, @file), :encoding => "utf-8")
